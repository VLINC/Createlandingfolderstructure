Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Victorious Love Pro Clean ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Victorious Love Pro Clean connects customers with professional cleaning service providers. We take data privacy seriously and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register as a customer or service provider, we collect:

  • Name and Contact Information: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Account Credentials: Username, password (encrypted with bcrypt)
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, business name (for providers)
  • Location Data: Service address, real-time GPS location for service delivery and tracking
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • Government ID: Driver's license, insurance documents (for provider verification and background checks)
  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, license plate (for providers)

2.2 Booking and Service Information

  • Service requests and bookings
  • Service history and completion status
  • Before/after photos uploaded by providers
  • Customer ratings and reviews
  • Real-time chat messages between customers and providers
  • Job acceptance/rejection history
  • Earnings and payment records

2.3 Mileage Tracking (Premium Feature)

For providers subscribed to our Mileage Tracker:

  • GPS Trip Data: Start/end coordinates, distance traveled
  • Trip Details: Date, time, purpose, notes
  • Tax Calculation: IRS-compliant mileage deductions
  • Exported Reports: CSV files for tax filing

2.4 Usage Information

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • IP address and general location
  • App usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Performance metrics
  • Push notification preferences

2.5 Location Information

We collect precise real-time location data when:

  • You request or provide a cleaning service
  • Providers are en route to your location
  • Active jobs are in progress (for customer tracking)
  • Mileage Tracker is active (GPS tracking for tax deduction purposes)

Note: Location tracking can be disabled in device settings, but this will limit core functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 To Provide Services

  • Match customers with available cleaning service providers
  • Process bookings and secure payments via Stripe
  • Enable real-time GPS location tracking during active jobs
  • Track business mileage for tax purposes (premium feature)
  • Facilitate in-app communication (booking messages, admin support chat)
  • Send booking confirmations, job updates, and notifications
  • Calculate provider earnings and process payouts

3.2 To Improve Services

  • Analyze usage patterns and service completion rates
  • Monitor app performance and identify bugs
  • Develop new features (mileage tracking, scheduling, etc.)
  • Conduct analytics on provider performance metrics
  • Improve matching algorithms

3.3 For Safety and Security

  • Verify provider identities through third-party background checks
  • Review uploaded documents (driver's license, insurance, certifications)
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Monitor compliance with platform policies
  • Enforce Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Resolve disputes between customers and providers
  • Investigate complaints and violations
  • Comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests

3.4 For Communication

  • Send booking confirmations and job status updates
  • Notify providers of new job offers
  • Alert customers when providers are en route
  • Provide customer support via in-app chat
  • Send account security notifications
  • Share promotional offers and updates (you can opt out)

3.5 For Tax and Financial Compliance

  • Generate 1099 forms for independent contractor providers
  • Store transaction history for IRS compliance (7 years)
  • Provide mileage reports for tax deduction purposes
  • Process cash-out requests and payouts

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Service Providers (When You Book)

Customers share with matched providers:

  • Full name and phone number
  • Service address and GPS coordinates
  • Booking details, service type, and special instructions
  • Real-time location during active service
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)

4.2 With Customers (When Providers Accept Jobs)

Providers share with customers:

  • Full name and business name
  • Profile photo and star rating
  • Phone number for direct contact
  • Real-time GPS location during job (en route and on-site)
  • Vehicle information

4.4 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with court orders, subpoenas, or legal processes
  • Respond to government requests or law enforcement
  • Protect our legal rights and enforce Terms of Service
  • Prevent fraud, illegal activity, or harm to users
  • Investigate safety incidents or policy violations

4.5 Business Transfers

In the event of merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity. You will be notified via email and in-app notification.

4.6 Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., "90% of bookings are completed on time") with business partners, researchers, and marketing partners. This data cannot identify you personally.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

5.2 Closed Accounts

  • Immediately Deleted: Profile photo, GPS location history, chat messages, mileage trip data
  • 90 Days: Backup copies (for disaster recovery and fraud prevention)
  • 7 Years: Financial records (IRS requirement for 1099 contractors)
  • Permanently Anonymized: Reviews and ratings (disassociated from your identity)

5.3 Legal Hold

Data may be retained longer if required for legal proceedings, tax audits, or regulatory compliance.

5.4 Mileage Data Retention

  • Active Subscriptions: Stored indefinitely while subscription is active
  • After Cancellation: Retained for 3 years for tax audit purposes
  • Manual Deletion: Available upon request (may affect tax compliance)

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Download

You have the right to view and download your data via Settings → Privacy & Data or request a copy via email.

6.2 Correction and Update

Update in-app (Settings → Edit Profile) or email privacy@proincorporated.services.

6.3 Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

To permanently delete your account, follow the in-app steps. Deletion is permanent and certain financial records will be retained for 7 years.

6.4 Opt-Out Rights

Opt out of marketing emails, push notifications, SMS, location tracking, and mileage tracking as described in-app.

6.5 Data Portability (GDPR)

EU residents can request their data in machine-readable format (JSON, CSV).

6.6 Object to Processing

You can object to automated decision-making, marketing, and analytics processing.

6.7 Restrict Processing

Request temporary restriction while we investigate a complaint.

7. Security Measures

7.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in Transit: TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at Rest: AES-256
  • Password Security: Bcrypt hashing with salting
  • Firebase Security Rules: Role-based access control
  • PCI-DSS Compliance: Stripe handles payment data
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Available for provider accounts
  • Secure Cloud Infrastructure: Google Firebase with redundancy

7.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Access Controls: Need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security reviews
  • Incident Response: 24-hour breach notification protocol
  • Employee Training: Annual security and privacy training
  • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary information

7.3 Your Responsibility

  • Use a strong, unique password (min. 8 characters)
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Log out on shared devices
  • Never share your password
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

7.4 Data Breach Notification

In case of a breach, we will notify you within 72 hours, provide details, remediation steps, and notify authorities as required.

8. Children's Privacy
